Deep Cleaning Services

Premium results, serviced by family. Deep cleaning is the reset that changes everything. When a home needs more than routine maintenance, we deliver detail-forward work that restores kitchens, bathrooms, floors, and high-touch areas to a higher standard.

When Deep Cleaning Makes Sense

  • Seasonal refresh (spring/fall resets)
  • First-time professional cleaning (establish a true baseline)
  • After heavy household traffic, hosting, or renovations
  • Any time you want a “fully finished” feel again

What’s Included in a Deep Clean

Kitchen Focus

  • Countertops sanitized and polished
  • Cabinet fronts wiped and detailed
  • Appliance exteriors cleaned
  • Sink and fixtures disinfected
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped

Bathroom Focus

  • Toilets thoroughly disinfected
  • Showers and tubs scrubbed
  • Tile surfaces cleaned
  • Mirrors polished
  • Fixtures shined

Living Areas & Bedrooms

  • Dusting of surfaces and décor
  • Vacuuming of carpets and rugs
  • Hard floor mopping
  • Trash removal

Frequently Asked Questions

How is deep cleaning different from regular cleaning?

Deep cleaning is detail-forward and designed to reset the home beyond maintenance. It’s ideal for first-time service, seasonal resets, and higher-impact results.

Do you offer recurring cleaning after a deep clean?

Yes. Many clients start with a deep clean and then move into a recurring schedule. See Residential Cleaning.

Is this the same as move-in/move-out?

Move-related service is its own scope. See Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning.
